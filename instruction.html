Instruction!

This experiemnt is to compare the performance of 2 different menus. Marking Menu and Radial Menu.

There will be 240 trials in total during the test. In each trial you will be given a target word. Please selected the corrected word using the promoted menu selction method.

The way to use marking menu and radio menu is as follow:

Marking Menu:

1. Popup: Left Mouse Down hold for 1 second

2. Select: Stroke to leaf node

3. Reset: Release Mouse Down

Note:* Expert user's can make a fast stroke instead of waiting for the manu to pop up.

Radial Menu:

1. Popup: Right Click

2. Select: Left Click

3. Reset: Right Click

Please write your participant number ID given by your experimenter. Do not take off your earphone during the entire test.
